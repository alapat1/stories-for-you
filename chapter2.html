✦   the other side   ✦
I know you've crossed bridges,
finding your way to me,
To bring your light
and show me the world.
I know I want to see you
on the other side,
having found the way to you,
To bring you the warmth I've yearned.
To brighten your day,
or lighten your night,
I know you're not always around,
day and night, each.
But always know that,
wherever you are,
My arms will always be in reach.
✦
crossing bridges
bringing light
finding the way
in reach
